Top Ten Songs (according to Cherisse.)

Songs personally picked, from all of my Spotify playlists.

What makes a song a good song, according to me?

  • The hook has to be catchy enough, no matter the genre.
  • I always like a song with a nice underlying meaning!
  • What's the personal significance to me or people I know? I like listening to songs that I, or others, have said that they can relate to.
